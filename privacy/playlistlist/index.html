All Playlistlist data is stored on-device, and does not ever leave your device, and it is never uploaded anywhere for any reason. The same goes for Playlistlist Max. Take a look at the privacy policies of our supported music providers, though:
  • Apple Music
  • Spotify
Some Playlistlist Social data (profile picture, username) is PUBLIC! When you use Playlistlist Social all of your playlists are uploaded to a database managed by me and can only be viewed by friends that you allow.
